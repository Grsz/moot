Barons Estate Agents have been established since 1988 and we are still proudly independently owned and run. When founded, our principles were to provide the highest level of service and dedication to our customers.

At Barons Estate Agents our commitment is not just to sell your property at top market value but to also provide you, the customer, with the highest level of service - from the point of our initial valuation through to completion.

With our experienced sales progression department not only do we sell your property but we ensure that the sale is as stress free as possible, liaising with solicitors, lenders and surveyors on your behalf and providing you with full updates and helpful advice throughout the transaction.

We are proud to be recognised licensed members of the NAEA, Trading standards and the Ombudsmen Scheme, giving you further re-assurance of our commitment to improving industry standards. Being part of the national association of independent estate agents we have over 800 associated offices meaning we have connections with the “london buyer” or a client looking to relocate from another region.

We understand that when selling or buying life's largest possession you need to be sure that you are receiving the highest level of service and advice.

All our staff at Barons are experienced in the art of negotiation

they receive regular and ongoing training to ensure that when an offer on your property is received we will negotiate the best possible sale price for you that fits around your time scales and commitments. Our staff live in the immediate area and even have children in the local schools, we know our clients market and understand what our clients want, and being owner-managed we can be flexible and make decisions in order to help you, our clients.

You can rest assured of Barons qualified professionalism, integrity and experience at all times and with our multifaceted modern technology and global coverage we will find you the right buyer. We will then ensure we qualify the buyer with a financial health check with our IFA to ensure the buyer has the ability to proceed before instructing solicitors, this reduces the chance of your sale falling through, giving you piece of mind.
